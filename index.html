Browser Tool Information 
            ### Browser Tool Information
            - **Purpose:** The browser tool allows you to look up real-time information, current events, and unfamiliar terms. It can open URLs provided by the user.
            - **Steps to Use:**
              1. **Search Query:** Issue a query to a search engine to display results.
              2. **Click Results:** Select at least 3 and at most 10 pages to retrieve their contents.
              3. **Write Response:** Summarize the results and provide citations.
              4. **Open URL:** Directly open a provided URL if specified by the user.
            - **Citation Format:**
              - Quotes: `【{message idx}†{link text}】`
              - Long Citations: `[link text](message idx)`

            **Location:** The browser tool is part of the assistant's toolkit and can be invoked when needed for tasks such as retrieving real-time information or exploring unfamiliar terms.
